A few words about me:

I am currently employed as a PostDoc by the Thuenen Institut for Biodiversity working on the MonVIA project focusing on developping methods to estimate farmland bird trends and the impact of changing agricultural practices. This work is hugely helped by inputs from the Dachverband Deutsche Avifaunister.

Between 2017 and 2019 I was a synthesis PostDoc by the university of Ghent working partly at TEREC and partly at ForNaLab within the TREEWEB. Before that I did my PhD at the Technical University of Munich with the Terrestrial ecology group and working on how plant diversity affect arthropod communities and the ecosystem functions that they perform. This was done within the framework of the Jena experiement, you can find the results from these 4 years of work at the mediaTUM website.
